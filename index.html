Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CRM Build ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as name, email, and login credentials).
  • Profile and business data you provide (customers, jobs, notes, invoices, reports, attachments).
  • Technical data (device type, app logs, and basic diagnostics).

2. Camera Permission

Our app may request access to your device camera (android.permission.CAMERA) to let you capture photos for app features (for example, job attachments, invoice images, or profile-related uploads).

  • Camera access is used only when you explicitly trigger a camera-related action.
  • We do not record video/audio in the background.
  • You can deny or revoke camera permission in your device settings at any time.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality.
  • To sync and store your data securely in backend services.
  • To improve reliability, security, and performance.
  • To send operational notifications (if enabled).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with service providers required to operate the app (for example, hosting, database, authentication, notifications, and payment providers), under contractual safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
